Powerball Numbers Generator

Here is a powerball number generator. It takes previous powerball historical data as a weighted reference. It should never repeat the combination of numbers.

WARNING: In order to have non-repeating tickets, please generate your batch all at once. Also, due to the method used, numbers that were never picked before as a winning number are excluded.
